Business Administration

Business Administration is the study of how organizations plan, organize, and control their resources to achieve their objectives. Students will learn about the different functional areas of business, including accounting, finance, marketing, and management. They will also learn about business ethics and social responsibility, and how to apply critical thinking and problem-solving skills to real-world business situations.
